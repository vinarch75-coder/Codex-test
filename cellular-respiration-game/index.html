Reference Cheat Sheet

Glycolysis

Location: Cytosol. Output: 2 ATP (net), 2 NADH, 2 pyruvate.

Pyruvate Oxidation

Location: Mitochondrial matrix. Output: 2 NADH, 2 acetyl-CoA, CO₂.

TCA Cycle

Location: Mitochondrial matrix. Output: 2 ATP (GTP), 6 NADH, 2 FADH₂.

ETC & Ox Phos

Location: Inner mitochondrial membrane. Output: ~26-28 ATP, H₂O.
